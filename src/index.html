NuLanes generates recurring revenue for cities through companies getting exposure on bike lanes

NuLanes connects cities and companies to make bike lane sponsorship simple and effective for both parties

  • First, NuLanes gains an understanding of the priorities of cities, companies and their respective stakeholders

  • Next, NuLanes pairs cities and companies based on preferences, and structures an equitable bike lane sponsorship agreement

  • Lastly, NuLanes ensures bike lane sponsorship terms are executed and manages relations between cities and companies thereafter
