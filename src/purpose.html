NuLanes thinks all cities should be travelable multimodally with safety & ease through the endorsement of companies that care about them.

Multimodal
Travel

With the U.S. having 67 million cyclists, bike lanes offer an inexpensive / environmentally considerate medium of travel.

Safety &
Ease

Bike lanes promote an easy / healthy way to traverse a city, while reducing overall cycling injuries and deaths which have been on the rise as cycling's popularity has increased.

Companies
that care

Bike lane sponsorship by companies reflects a targeted investment into environmental / social enhancement in cities which stakeholders care about.
